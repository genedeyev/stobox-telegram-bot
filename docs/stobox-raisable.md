# Stobox Raisable — the "Raise" layer

Stobox Raisable is the second of Stobox's three layers
(**Intelligence → Raisable → Compass**). It turns the verified record built in
Stobox Intelligence into **a broker-accepted regulated offering** — generating
the offering documents from your canonical record. Tagline: **"Get known. Get
funded. Keep more of what you raise."**

Source: https://www.stobox.io/raisable

## What it does (the problems it solves)

- **Documents generated from your record** — instead of months of blank-page
  legal drafting, the package is pre-filled from your Stobox Intelligence record,
  with every figure consistent.
- **Flat fees — never a percentage.** No placement agent taking a cut of the raise.
- **Every exemption mapped & prepared** across regulatory frameworks.
- **Broker access** — routed to vetted broker-dealer partners, matched to counsel.

## The raise process (4 steps)

1. **Package & data room** — drawn from your Intelligence record.
2. **Prepare each exemption** — as separate 90-day windows.
3. **Self-directed or broker** — depending on the exemption.
4. **Route the deal & close** — through a licensed broker-dealer.

## The document package it generates

PPM, Subscription Agreement, Form D, Investor Questionnaire, Risk Factors, and a
Data Room — pre-filled from your Stobox Intelligence record, every figure
consistent.

## Supported offering frameworks

The platform prepares materials across these frameworks (as listed on the live site):

- **US:** Reg D 506(b) / 506(c), Reg CF (crowdfunding), Reg A+ (mini-IPO), Reg S (offshore).
- **International:** EU Prospectus Regulation, UK FCA (POATRs), Switzerland FinSA.

**Compliance guardrail (important):** Stoby lists which frameworks Raisable
*supports*, but **never states which exemption or framework applies to any
specific offering, company, or token** — that depends on the offering's own
documents and the issuer's counsel. If asked "which one applies to me?", route to
the team and their counsel; do not decide it.
