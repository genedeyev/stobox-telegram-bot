# Stobox Intelligence — the "Organize" layer

Stobox Intelligence is the first of Stobox's three layers
(**Intelligence → Raisable → Compass**). It turns a private company into **one
canonical, verifiable company record, scored by AXIS** — described as "the
operating brain of your company": a living record that reads your documents,
reconciles them into one canonical truth, scores how ready you are, and stays
current as the business changes.

Source: https://www.stobox.io/intelligence

## What it does

### One canonical record
- Every figure in one place, **with a source attached**.
- **340+ canonical data points** — the same structure Stobox itself runs on.
- Every data point is **typed**, carries a **status** — Verified, Partial,
  Assumption, or Unverified — and **links back to the document it came from**.

### Contradiction & gap engine
- "It finds the problems before your investor's lawyer does."
- Automatic cross-checks across all the data; flags unsupported figures and
  missing information; when sources conflict, it shows **both** conflicting
  sources.

### Secure data room
- Three visibility tiers: **private, conditional, public**.
- Every view and download is **logged**; scoped access instead of bulk file
  sharing; **one-click diligence export**.

## The AXIS Readiness Score

Intelligence scores the record across **7 weighted AXIS pillars** (AXIS = Asset
eXperience Intelligence Score — the open methodology also used by Stobox Compass):

| Pillar | Weight |
|---|---|
| Asset | 20 |
| Legal | 18 |
| Transparency | 17 |
| Technology | 15 |
| Liquidity | 15 |
| Governance | 10 |
| Risk | 5 |

**Important framing:** the AXIS score is **not a credit rating or an endorsement**.
It measures how **complete** and how **verified** your record is — nothing more.

## How to get started

No long implementation: **upload what you have, and Intelligence does the
structuring.** Four steps: **Connect & upload → Reconcile → Score → Stay live.**
The **free Readiness Score** (25 questions, no credit card) is the easiest entry
point. Start in the product at https://app.stobox.io or read more at
https://www.stobox.io/intelligence.
